History and Legal Status

Petoma Works Ltd was founded in March 2007, initially as a sole proprietorship but Restructured later to respond to expanded workload and changing operating environment.

The firm is to date, a limited liability company with the majority shares held by the professional engineers of the firm. After its formation in 2007, the firm initially concentrated on buildings. Since then the firm has expanded its engineering designs and construction services in the other types of civil engineering works, including transportation and infrastructure. The range of clients has expanded correspondingly to encompass private clients, public departments, and other contractors. The firm has since 2011 been incorporated as a share holding company with limited liability.

The Directors of the firm hold all the shares. The company has had a steady and a gradual growth since inception with the total permanent staff growth rising from 2 in 2007 to 22 to date.

Mission

Is to provide best ICT consultancy services to the private and telecoms sectors focusing on networks design, infrastructure and systems audit, security and ICT policy frameworks implementation.

To provide construction services, construction consultancy services, tools, machinery and equipment.

To provide poles for electricity and general wood provisions to better the lives of our customers.
